march 28th, 2025

i broke my ipod again but that's a blog for a later date

a little while ago, i created a page for my thoughts

i'm not linking it here for reasons but if you're curious about this page poke around my about me
i created this page to replace how i used twitter, as twitter really messes with my head

whenever posting on my private account (or even my main account but to a lesser extent), i get really anxious if nobody likes or replies to it
my brain interprets no interaction as people not caring

however, when posting here, i don't have that issue!
i can't worry about people not interacting with my post because they don't care - i know that people won't see it because they don't know i posted
i can't worry about posting something stupid, because i'm not competing with people to create interesting content here

despite the fact that less people will look at these posts, i feel more seen here

probably because i'm not comparing what attention i get to what other people are getting
